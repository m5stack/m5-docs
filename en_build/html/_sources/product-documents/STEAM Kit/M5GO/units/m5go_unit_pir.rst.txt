M5GO PIR
========

DESCRIPTION
-----------

The M5GO PIR is a human infrared sensor for M5GO that can easily detect
whether someone appears in front of it with M5GO Core.

FEATURES
--------

-  Detects the distance 150cm
-  Two Lego installation holes
